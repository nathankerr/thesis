\chapter{RESULTS}

This chapter contains the results and analysis of the experiments
previously described. The results have been grouped into two major areas:
record level operations and dataset level operations. It is expected
that PostGIS will outperform hadoopGIS and clusterGIS in the record
level operations. HadoopGIS and clusterGIS should outperform PostGIS on
dataset level operations.
