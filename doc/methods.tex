\begin{abstract}
Databases provide good interfaces for interactive or programatic data analysis and exploration. However, the large datasets generated by modern parallel applications make executing database queries in an interactive timeframe less and less tenable. This paper looks at methodologies of executing parallel queries in databases with the intent of making such queries both powerful and simple.
\end{abstract}
